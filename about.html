About me

Renata

I am a female photographer based in Amsterdam, and my photography is a tribute to women.

I am always willing to travel for my work. I mostly shoot close to nature, capturing the authenticity of our wildness and connection to the earth. I work with Canon digital cameras as well as analog ones to create specific vibrations.

My love for women's photos began in college when I graduated from the Art Academy in Poznan. For my bachelor's defense, I worked on the "5 sisters project." Years later, completely spontaneously with a group of friends, we did a photoshoot for our 30th birthdays. As time passed, I managed to change my profession, but I always felt a strong longing to photograph and work with women.

I am an open, trusting, and creative spirit. I enjoy discovering emotions and trusting my intuition. During a session with me, I can make you feel completely comfortable. It can be a "hot" session or a cozy experience, like being wrapped in a blanket with tea by the fireplace or enjoying a glass of wine in the bathtub.
